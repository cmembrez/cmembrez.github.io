Augmented Reality Escape Room

❮

Formative study built upon a series of 15 meetings in collaboration with the FSA. The image is a conventional pen and paper rehabilitation task, which we try to understand how they may be gamified using an AR headset, and whether they may be usable and accessible by people with visual impairments.

Brainstorming to come up with three mockups.

Example of tasks implemented.

A "Creation" menu exists to load and save markers on which the tasks will be placed. Spacial mapping is currently not used.

Virtual Reality Adventures Game

❮ ❯

This is my first video game and development using virtual reality with the Unity game engine.

I used Blender to create each low poly assets such as sword, houses and monsters,

as well as GIMP to model some of the textures for the floor and walls.

This made me acquire a great global knowledge on game development methods and each chain of its applications. This was a semester project at university. Due to hardware issue (my GPU was not supported anymore during development), the video game was presented as WebGL during the end of the semester.

Object-oriented programming for a 2D game

❮ ❯

Play as Ludwig and crush the invaders with your music notes and avoid the flats from the aliens.

Three difficulties are available through a drop-down menu. Also, once all aliens are killed, you reach a new level with enemies' speed increased.

A 2D Java game based on a Model-view-controller (MVC) pattern. More details on the GitHub repo.
