Augmented Reality Escape Room

❮

Through a collaboration with an association for people with visual impairments (FSA), an exploratory study is conducted based on conventional tasks to understand how they may be gamified using an AR headset, and whether they may be usable and accessible by people with visual impairments.

As such, 3 AR tasks have been developed in the form of an escape room. They can be solved using any of two interaction modalities: touch to press buttons, or air tap to pinch and slide sliders.

A "Creation" menu exists to load and save markers on which the tasks will be placed. Spacial mapping is currently not used.

Virtual Reality Adventures Game

❮ ❯

This is my first video game and development using virtual reality with the Unity game engine.

I used Blender to create each low poly assets such as sword, houses and monsters,

as well as GIMP to model some of the textures for the floor and walls.

This made me acquire a great global knowledge on game development methods and each chain of its applications. This was a semester project at university. Due to hardware issue (my GPU was not supported anymore during development), the video game was presented as WebGL during the end of the semester.
